Travel Safe-Vaccination Map

Vaccination recommendations depend on the type of trip planned and how long it will take. Based on this and the risk of infection, trips are divided into four groups:
  • Group 1
  • Business or conference trips to the capital or larger cities for a few days.
  • Group 2
  • Tourist trips of up to 4 weeks duration, visiting countryside with poor hygiene conditions.
  • Group 3
  • Trips of several months or visiting relatives.
  • Group 4
  • Stays of more than half a year or repeated visits to a risk area.
